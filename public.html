Important info BEFORE you Sign up:

Pre-Requisites

  • Speak & read English fluently.

  • Minimum age is 18 / maximum age is 70.
  • No prior racing experience is needed.
  • A basic ability with a manual transmission is MANDATORY ! (this is NOT a good place to learn it or “brush up” on your rusty shifting skills).
  • Most people will fit into the formula race car; if you are 6’4 & 275 lbs you may still fit (email us for a pdf copy of our “at home fitment test” to be sure: kim@formulacarthrill.events)
  • Participants need to sign liability waivers.
  • A currently valid driver’s licence is not required.

Please Read All of These

  • We are located at the “Driver Development Center” at Canadian Tire Motorsport Park just north of Bowmanville (only a few minutes north of Hwy 407).
  • Driver’s do not have any Formula Racecar damage responsibility (but we insist on respect being given to our racecars).
  • Lunch is provided for drivers only (spectators should bring their own food and drinks… there is NOT a food / concession stand on site).
  • Helmet / racing suit / other racing gear are provided free of charge (although participants are responsible for any damage or loss to them).
  • Enrollment cancellations are done free of charge up to 60 days prior to Event date (any later than that will have a 20% cancellation fee applied).
  • We will apply any Covid safety protocols as necessary.
  • Should the track’s local municipality have a Covid restriction that prevents the running of the Event, full refunds will be made for all participants.
  • For the safety of everyone involved, we reserve the right to preclude participation due to any physical or mental limitation or disability.
  • We absolutely do not allow any sort of on board (go pro type) camera nor any sort of drone – no exceptions – expulsion will result for a lack of adherence.
  • “How fast will you go?”… short answer is fast enough to be thrilling and slow enough to help extend our 37 year injury free safety record! One of the great things about FormulaCars is that they feel very fast when you drive them (you sit very low to the ground with the engine noise right behind you and the wind whistling by your helmet!) and therefore the experience is awesome but you are not going Indycar speeds of 200 mph!
  • “Do I get to race or pass other cars?”… even in full blown racing school Racing Licence Courses it takes several days to get drivers to the point of the racing and passing part of the curriculum… the good news is the challenge of piloting a very capable FormulaCar on a real racing track is an incredible experience itself and one you will remember forever!

Important Info AFTER you Sign Up:

  • What do I need to bring?
  • We suggest a t-shirt / pair of gym shorts / and shoes that are a bit “form fitting” (not big huge wide ones) that lace up and don’t have a big soft cushy sole (so soft cushy running shoes to help absorb impact aren’t good for feeling the pedals of your race car).
  • Spectators & Kids & Pets: you will be responsible for the conduct / behaviour (we reserve the right to have spectators leave the property if we require them to).
  • Please note food and drinks we supply are for drivers only (and there are no provisions nor snack stand on site for spectators)… Pontypool is the closest little town that may have food / drinks available… otherwise, there’s a Tim Horton’s out on hwy 35 / 115 about 10 minutes from the track.
  • The trackside Event Center is mostly dedicated to the drivers, but spectators are welcome to use the washrooms and the building for shelter but we do ask that they respect facilities and not be disruptive to the course that being taught / run.
  • Any one is welcome to take photos or videos from any view point that is not part of the actual race track area (and spectators are only allowed in pit lane when the cars are off track and all engines are shut off).
